What is Telehealth?

Telehealth allows a patient to consult a doctor or healthcare provider remotely via video call or phone when a physical examination is unnecessary, and/or the patient is unable to present to a clinic in person. Bharat Health provides telehealth via video calls and online requests.

How is patient information secured?

Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers located in Australia. All patient data and user data is encrypted at rest and in transit using enterprise-grade encryption protocols.

Data in transit: Movement of data between the webserver and browser is secured with the latest HTTPS technology using RSA encrypted TLS (Transport Layer Security).

Data storage: All user and patient data is stored on secure Amazon Web Services servers (AWS Sydney). These servers conform to the ISO27001 standard ensuring data remains backed up and safe at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

More FAQ's
What is Bharat Health?

Bharat Health is an Australian 24 hour on-demand telehealth provider. This means you can speak with an AHPRA registered GP for advice, prescriptions or referrals, or request and receive an approved medical certificate via our online form, within 15 minutes, 7 days a week from wherever you are.

What healthcare services does Bharat Health offer?
Bharat Health offers
– Telehealth doctor consultations ($35 short consultations, $45 long consultations)
– Online medical certificates ($15.90 single day certificates, $35 multiday certificates)
– Online prescriptions ($17.90 single script requests, multiple script requests available via a short consultation with a doctor)
– Pathology referrals (via a short consultation with a doctor)
– Radiology referrals (via a short consultation with a doctor)
Is Bharat Health Australian?

Bharat Health is an Australian owned and operated health-tech business, founded in Perth in 2022 under the parent brand UIET Pty Ltd. Bharat Health works exclusively with Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA) accredited doctors.

Is Bharat Health Safe?

Bharat Health is safe – all consults, referrals, scripts and medical certificates are provided by Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA) accredited doctors. All patient data is stored on secure, encrypted servers located in Australia which run on Australian IRAP certified infrastructure (AWS Sydney). All patient data and user data is encrypted at rest and in transit using enterprise-grade encryption protocols.
